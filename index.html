📐 Normalization Forms Applied

        

          

            
1NF — First Normal Form
All columns are atomic. No repeating groups. Each cell has a single value.

            
2NF — Second Normal Form
No partial dependencies. Every non-key column depends on the full primary key.

            
3NF — Third Normal Form
No transitive dependencies. Customer data stored once in customers table.

            
BCNF — Boyce-Codd
Every determinant is a candidate key. Schema fully satisfies BCNF requirements.
